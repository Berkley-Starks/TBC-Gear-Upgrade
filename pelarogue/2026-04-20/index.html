Hit cap note

You are at 188 hit rating / 11.92%, vs. the 63-rating yellow-cap. That's ~125 rating of dead stat. Not a fix — just context. When evaluating future upgrades, mentally discount any +hit value to zero. Netherblade Chestpiece's +22 hit, Violet Signet's +25 hit, Bladefist's Breadth's hit — all overcap. If you see a replacement with equivalent AP/Agi but no hit, take it.
