## Headline finding: spec / weapon mismatch

**Malchazeen is a dagger**, but your talents are **Sword Specialization** (20/41/0). This is the single biggest DPS loss on the sheet.

| You are losing | Why |
|---|---|
| Sword Specialization 5/5 (5% chance extra attack) | Procs on swords only |
| Weapon Expertise 2/2 (+5 MH expertise) | Swords / fists only — daggers excluded |
| Human sword racial (+5 MH expertise) | Swords / maces only |

Net: roughly ~10 wasted MH expertise + zero Sword Spec procs. The cap table confirms it — MH expertise sits at 10/25 while OH (King's Defender, a sword) is 15/20. Estimated loss is **8–12% DPS** depending on armor and buff state. Everything else on this audit is secondary to fixing the MH.

Replace with [enchant]Enchant Chest — Exceptional Stats[/enchant] (+6 all stats). Nets ~+6 Agi + AP/Hit/Crit scaling from rogue talents. Self-apply.

## Hit cap note

You are at **188 hit rating / 11.92%**, vs. the 63-rating yellow-cap. That's ~125 rating of dead stat. Not a fix — just context. When evaluating future upgrades, mentally discount any `+hit` value to zero. Netherblade Breastplate's +22 hit, Violet Signet's +25 hit, Bladefist's Breadth's hit — all overcap. If you see a replacement with equivalent AP/Agi but no hit, take it.
